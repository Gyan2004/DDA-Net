Q: How long does training take?
A: ~2-3 hours per model on GPU, CPU takes much longer

Q: Which model should I choose?
A: DDANet (best balance) or U-Net (fastest)

Q: Out of memory error?
A: Reduce batch size: --batch-size 4

Q: Loss not decreasing?
A: Try lower learning rate: --lr 5e-4

Q: Can I resume training?
A: Yes, checkpoints auto-load. Just run train.py again

Q: How do I compare models?
A: Run: python evaluate_models.py (after training)

Q: Can I use this on my own data?
A: Yes, just place images in images/ and masks/ folders
